3. App Purpose and Functionality

Italia Permesso allows users to check the status of their Permesso di Soggiorno (Italian residence permit) using a 12-character tracking number. The app retrieves the status from the official website of the Questura (Italian Police Department). This tool is designed for informational purposes and helps users avoid the hassle of visiting the website manually each time.
